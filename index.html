About us

Carefully thought of in the dawn of 2015 with the primary motive of promoting cutting-edge technology, encouraging innovation and fostering creativity, Tachyon 2018 has been initiated by the Computer Society of India JUET Chapter and the CSE Department to bring out the best from the brains of our university.
Our mission, through this series, is not only to inspire budding engineers to explore, discover and connect with the endless world of renowned personalities and myriad possibilities, but to go beyond limits (faster than light, maybe) and find solutions to their banal yet significant problems.
Tachyon is an endeavor to provide an outstanding platform for the students to showcase their talent and skillsets in fiercest of competitions. Tachyon strives for one and all to get inspired and make an effort to change the world.

Kodeathon

Kodeathon is an online programming contest series conducted by the Programming Club of the CSI, student branch of JUET. It brings together the students who share their interest in coding with other students to create, connect, compete and test their mathematical and logical problem solving skills.
