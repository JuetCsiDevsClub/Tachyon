The Brief:

Kodeathon International is an online algorithmic programming contest series conducted by the Programming Club of the CSI, student branch of JUET. It brings together the students who share their interest in coding with other students to create, connect, compete and test their mathematical and logical problem solving skills. The individual participants have to crack the stated problems by solving computer programming. This competition will be conducted on hackerearth.

Task:

The individual participants have to write an efficient program which solves the given problem in the given time frame and produces the desired output.
